Motivation

I created this project because I wanted to build something that would have use and impact in my life.
My family loves hiking, and I am starting to get into it as well- there are a lot of resources to see lists of hikes that people have been on, but I wanted to try making one for myself.
I chose to use GIS mapping so that I could see relative locations of the hiking trails, and I chose to display information factors via GIS pop-ups that would impact hiking difficulty.
